About Me

I am Alma, a designer who enjoys combining playful objects and everyday scenes with digital experiences. These details inspire how I think about texture, balance and storytelling in my work. I focus on creating interfaces that feel honest, gentle and intuitive, while still carrying a clear personality.

Education

I completed a Higher Diploma at Hong Kong Design Institute, specialising in Visual Communication (UX Design). During these years, I explored user journeys, information architecture and storytelling through interface.

Studio projects trained me to move from research and sketching to wireframes and high‑fidelity prototypes, always testing how people actually feel when they use a product. This foundation shaped the way I think about clarity, hierarchy and emotional tone in every screen.
