Poster for VITA

Poster for VITA, created with Adobe Illustrator. Heavy shadow and a Muji‑inspired graphic style are used to keep the layout simple but strong, highlighting the drink in a quiet, calm way.

Refreshment Break

This poster highlights VITA as a freshness and relaxing break for students from their busy study schedule. The composition and colour focus on that small pause in the day where you can breathe, reset and feel refreshed again.

The Colour Red

A poster that describes the colour red – how red can represent different emotions and vibes at the same time: vintage yet lively, warm but intense. The design plays with these contrasts through texture, type and layered shapes.
