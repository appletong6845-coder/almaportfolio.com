UX Project 1

This project is a pixel‑style fitness app that uses gamification to motivate design students to stay active. By turning workouts into small quests, collecting rewards and unlocking tiny pixel worlds, the app makes movement feel playful instead of stressful.

UX Project 2

This project focuses on a HKMOA membership app that enhances customer relationship management. The goal is to provide a cleaner, more intuitive system that organises benefits, bookings and notifications clearly, while empowering staff to deliver membership services with less friction.

UX Project 3

This project introduces a VR gamification experience that provides VTC career information during the VTC Open Day. By presenting programmes and pathways inside an interactive game world, visitors can explore options more easily and understand complex career information in a playful, memorable way.
